Meet the Owner

Hi, my name is Zoe. I started Grandma's Closet back in 2015. I went to college at UWEC and loved shopping at all the small businesses in downtown. I realized there wasn't a size-inclusive vintage store, so once I graduated I decided to start my own. I've been collecting vintage clothes ever since I was a young girl. The older I get, the more I realize that not only is buying pre-loved clothing fun, its crucial to keep our world clean. I'd love for you to stop by and add some new staples to your wardrobe!
